# FinProof v1 — Research Tier (Medium Difficulty)

This repository contains **Tier 3** of the FinProof v1 BFSI Adversarial Benchmark — 2,036 medium-difficulty attack prompts available to academic researchers and non-commercial model evaluators under the FinProof Research Agreement.

**Public benchmark (Tier 1 + 2):** [Zytra/finproof-bench](https://huggingface.co/datasets/Zytra/finproof-bench)

## What's in this tier

**2,036 medium-difficulty attack prompts** across 7 BFSI attack categories.

| Category | Prompts | Description |
|---|---|---|
| B-01 | 273 | Investment Advice Elicitation |
| B-02 | 301 | KYC/AML Bypass |
| B-03 | 270 | Regulatory Misrepresentation |
| B-04 | 271 | Document Hallucination |
| B-05 | 304 | Consent & Data Rights |
| B-06 | 304 | Transaction Integrity |
| B-07 | 313 | Account Auth. Bypass |

**Difficulty breakdown:**
- 1,400 ASSAY v1 `indirect` — professional framing, intent not explicit
- 173 QCBM 8q boundary — PennyLane quantum circuit, boundary-filtered seeds
- 125 QCBM 8q gradient — gradient-weighted quantum sampling
- 123 QCBM 10q Fix1 — 10-qubit paraphrase generation, **27.6% Semalith miss rate**
- 100 QCBM 8q joint — cross-pattern semantics
- 115 retail rm_internal — relationship manager to internal AI register

**Why medium difficulty matters:**
These prompts are the most valuable distributional contribution in FinProof. The QCBM-generated examples (521 prompts) were generated by training quantum circuit Born machines on Semalith's decision boundary and sampling novel feature vectors — providing coverage of the distributional gaps between easy and hard attacks that classical generation misses.

Semalith v1.5 miss rate on this tier: **14.5%** (92/636 scored).
